SUB nivis

SUB Nivis is a retro doominspired shooter that has been made in our Custom Engine called (TheOtherEngine / TOE). The engine makes use of the Quake Level editor for level design & we made use out of our built in editor for debugging & testing.

Gameplay structure makes complete use of the Entity Component System, made possible by the Entt API.
