About me
Hey there! Thank you for visiting my Portfolio site. My name is Guy, I'm a 24-year-old developer from the Netherlands.
While Growing up, I became fascinated with retro games such as the old Zeldas, Runescape, Donkey Kong 64 and Fable Lost-Chapters. I grew up playing a lot of those alongside my older brother, which resulted in me dabbling in programming for the first time at the age of 12. I did this by customizing my first Runescape Private Server in Java.
I completely fell in love with programming and have continued since then. I have earned two degrees & completed two internships, and am still doing what I love most. I have a passion for discovering & learning new things, and applying my newfound knowledge where possible!
