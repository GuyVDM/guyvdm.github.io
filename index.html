About me
Hey there! Thank you for visiting my Portfolio site. My name is Guy, I'm a 24 year old developer from the Netherlands.
Growing up alongside my older siblings I became fascinated with retro games such as the old Zeldas, Runescape, DK, Fable LC, you name it. I grew up playing alot of those alongside my older brother which ended up in me making my first move into the field of programming at age 12, when I tried to customize my first Runescape Private Server in Java.
Ever since then it has been snowballing, 2 courses & internships further and here I am, still doing what I love most. I have a passion for discovering & learning new things, and apply my newfound knowledge where possible!
